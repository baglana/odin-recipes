Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is easy to make and full of flavor.
It tastes good hot or cold!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasoning
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto
    and seasonings.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta
    and toss with cheese.
